About The Weather Spectrum

Our Mission: To provide accessible, accurate weather information and innovative tools that help people understand and prepare for weather events affecting their communities.

What We Do

The Weather Spectrum is a comprehensive weather information platform offering real-time forecasts, severe weather alerts, and interactive visualization tools. We bridge the gap between complex meteorological data and everyday users who need practical weather information.

Unlike paid weather services, we believe critical weather information should be freely accessible to everyone. Our platform aggregates data from trusted sources like the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration (NOAA) and the National Weather Service, presenting it in user-friendly formats that anyone can understand.

Data Sources

The Weather Spectrum aggregates weather information from authoritative public sources, including:

  • National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration (NOAA)
  • National Weather Service (NWS)
  • Storm Prediction Center (SPC)
  • Other publicly available meteorological data feeds

We do not generate our own weather forecasts. We present data from these trusted agencies in formats designed for clarity and usability.

Important Disclaimer

While we strive for accuracy, The Weather Spectrum is an informational resource and should not replace official weather warnings or professional meteorological advice. For life-safety decisions, always consult official sources like your local National Weather Service office or emergency management agency.
